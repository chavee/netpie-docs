parseObj()
==========

Parse OBJ lines into model. For reference, this is what a simple model of a
square might look like:
v -0.5 -0.5 0.5
v -0.5 -0.5 -0.5
v -0.5 0.5 -0.5
v -0.5 0.5 0.5
f 4 3 2 1
