rotate()
========

rotate our Matrix around an axis by the given angle.

**รูปแบบการใช้งาน**

rotate ( a, axis )

**พารามิเตอร์**

- ``a``  Number: The angle of rotation in radians

- ``axis``  p5.Vector,Array: the axis(es) to rotate around
