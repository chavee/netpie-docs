inverseTranspose()
==================

converts a 4x4 matrix to its 3x3 inverse tranform
commonly used in MVMatrix to NMatrix conversions.

**รูปแบบการใช้งาน**

inverseTranspose ( mat4 )

**พารามิเตอร์**

- ``mat4``  : the matrix to be based on to invert
